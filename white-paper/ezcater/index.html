ItsaCheckmate helps restaurants access high value business customers with ezCater integration

ItsaCheckmate, the leading platform that integrates multiple online ordering services into point-of-sale (POS) systems, will now integrate orders originating from the ezCater platform directly into the POS systems of restaurants.

ezCater is the world’s largest catering marketplace and helps restaurants of all sizes reach high-value business customers and grow their catering business. By bringing catering online, ezCater gives businesspeople a simple, reliable way to order food from over 70,000 restaurants nationwide.

The ItsaCheckmate integration with ezCater has been a game changer for the operations, and overall profitability of my restaurant. My employees no longer have to manually enter or reconcile lengthy ezCater orders, which reduces errors upon delivery.

Eddie Martin

Jet's Pizza Franchisee

ItsaCheckmate’s full-service business model includes menu management and revenue reconciliation across all Off-Premise ordering platforms used by a restaurant. ItsaCheckmate has built direct integrations with over 35 different Point of Sale systems, including Par Brink, Heartland Restaurant, Revel, Toast, NCR Aloha in addition to many others.

Now, ezCater orders can integrate directly into these POS systems and eliminate errors that can occur with manual entry. Catering orders are typically placed days in advance of their actual delivery date. The integration with ItsaCheckmate allows restaurant operators to choose when to process ezCater orders to sync to their current operational and fiscal schedule.

ItsaCheckmate can integrate ezCater directly into more than 35 POS systems, helping restaurants of all sizes to efficiently manage and grow their catering business.Catering is a huge growth driver for restaurants, and this partnership helps them take full advantage of that opportunity.
